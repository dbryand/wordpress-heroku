Why should I care?

Besides giving our furry loved ones a great place to romp and swim, the ocean provides 50% of our oxygen (literally the air we breathe), 97% of the world’s water, protein for over a billion people, medical advancements, and $500 billion in global economy.

Bad news

The ocean is at risk due to plastics, pollution, climate change, over-fishing, etc. The Pacific Ocean is the world’s largest landfill with an estimated 3-5million tons of trash. We may not be throwing our poop bags directly into the ocean, but we are polluting the ocean and environment with our waste.

Good news

There are things we can do to help…use flushable dog poop bags, carry re-usable water bottles, bringing bags to the market, carpooling or taking public transportation, eating local foods. Learn more at The Ocean and You.
